Selected Work

Brands built to last

Brand Identity

NovuKraft Finance

The Problem

A fintech startup launching into a saturated market with no visual identity. Their offering was sophisticated but they appeared indistinguishable from competitors.

The Solution

Developed a sharp, trust-forward identity — a monogram mark combining stability with forward motion, paired with a precise sans-serif and a deep teal-navy palette.

The Result

A complete brand system across digital and print. Client secured seed funding within two months of launch, citing brand confidence as a key factor.

Brand Identity

Amara Artisan Foods

The Problem

An artisan food brand relaunching with premium products but carrying outdated packaging and a visual identity that no longer reflected their elevated quality.

The Solution

A warm, handcrafted identity rooted in Nigerian heritage — earthy gold tones, a custom wordmark with organic letterforms, and a brand story woven into every touchpoint.

The Result

Successful relaunch into premium retail channels. 3x engagement increase on social media within the first quarter post-rebrand.

Brand Identity

Zenith Creative Agency

The Problem

A new creative agency needed to differentiate in a crowded space, attract high-value clients, and project authority without an established portfolio.

The Solution

A geometric, forward-pointing diamond mark symbolising peak performance and precision. Midnight palette with electric teal accents — distinctive, modern, memorable.

The Result

Landed three enterprise clients in the first month of operation. Brand identity became a portfolio showpiece, earning recognition in design circles.
